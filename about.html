About Us

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to excellence

Welcome to BT/BC Oddamavadi Central College

BT/BC Oddamavadi Central College, established as a National School, has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in the Eastern Province of Sri Lanka. For over seven decades, we have been shaping young minds and preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of our nation.

Our school is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education. We provide a nurturing environment where students from diverse backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and excel in their chosen fields.

With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a comprehensive curriculum, we ensure that every student receives the best possible education. Our alumni have gone on to achieve remarkable success in various fields, making significant contributions to society both nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral values. We are committed to developing well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and character to succeed in an ever-changing world and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to achieve academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. We envision a future where our students become innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and active contributors to building a better world for all.
