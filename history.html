Our History

109 years of excellence in education and community service (1917 - 2026)

A Historic Landmark of Learning

BT/BC Oddamavadi Central College stands as one of the oldest and most prestigious educational institutions in Oddamavadi and the Batticaloa District. Established around 1917, the school is proudly celebrating 109 years of educational excellence from 1917 to 2026.

Over more than a century, the school has evolved from a small local institution into a National School under the Ministry of Education, recognized for its outstanding academic standards, comprehensive facilities, and commitment to holistic student development.

Role in the Community

BT/BC Oddamavadi Central College is not just an educational institution — it is a historic landmark and pillar of the Oddamavadi community. Alongside other important schools such as Oddamavadi Fathima Balika Maha Vidyalayam and Sharif Ali Vidyalaya, it forms the core educational network of the area.

The school regularly engages in awareness campaigns, sports meets, cultural events, student development programs, and community service activities, demonstrating that its contribution extends far beyond classroom teaching.

Over its 109-year history, the school has witnessed the transformation of Oddamavadi from a rural village into a developed town, and has produced countless professionals, teachers, government officers, and community leaders who continue to serve society with distinction.

🏫 1917

Foundation

BT/BC Oddamavadi Central College was established to serve the educational needs of the local community, marking the beginning of over a century of educational excellence.

📖 1930s-40s

Early Growth

The school expanded its facilities and curriculum, establishing itself as a key educational institution in Oddamavadi and the Batticaloa District.

🔬 1950s-60s

Modern Development

Introduction of modern teaching methods and the first science laboratory, marking a significant milestone in providing quality science education.

🏆 1970

National School Status

The school was elevated to National School status by the Ministry of Education in recognition of its outstanding contribution to education.

🏗️ 1985

New School Building

Construction of the modern school building was completed, providing state-of-the-art facilities for a growing student population.

💻 1992

Computer Education

The school became one of the first in the region to introduce computer education, preparing students for the digital age.

🚀 2024

STEM Excellence Center

Opening of the state-of-the-art STEM Excellence Center equipped with modern laboratories for advanced scientific education.

🎉 2026

109 Years Anniversary

Celebrating 109 years of educational contribution (1917 to 2026), continuing the legacy as a pillar of regional development.
